TitleWave

TitleWave is a Chrome extension that helps people choose better titles for their Stack Overflow questions.
It was built in four weeks as part of the Insight Data Science Fellowship program.
See the github page: https://github.com/tennessejoyce/Stack-Exchange-Title-Generator.
I use Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to both evaluate titles, and to suggest
new titles given the text of the question. Specifically, I trained two deep neural networks
with BERT and T5 architectures on hundreds of thousands of Stack Overflow questions.

---Tennesse Joyce
